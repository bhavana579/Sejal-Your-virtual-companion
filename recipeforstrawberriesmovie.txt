**Strawberry Bliss Parfait**

Inspired by the sweetness of life, just like the movie "Strawberry"

 Servings: 4-6

Ingredients:

* 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
* 1 cup vanilla yogurt
* 1/2 cup granola
* 1/4 cup honey
* 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips (at least 70% cocoa)
* 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional)
* Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. In a small bowl, mix together the honey and heavy cream (if using). Set aside.
2. In a separate bowl, melt the dark chocolate chips in the microwave or over a double boiler. Let it cool slightly.
3. In a large glass or parfait dish, create a layer of vanilla yogurt. You can use a piping bag or simply spoon it in.
4. Add a layer of sliced strawberries on top of the yogurt. You can arrange them in a pattern or simply scatter them.
5. Sprinkle a layer of granola over the strawberries. This adds a nice crunch to the dish.
6. Drizzle the honey-cream mixture (if using) or a drizzle of honey over the granola.
7. Repeat the layers two more times, ending with a layer of yogurt on top.
8. Drizzle the melted dark chocolate over the top layer of yogurt in a zig-zag pattern.
9. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and serve chilled.

**Tips and Variations:**

* Use fresh strawberries for the best flavor and texture. You can also use frozen strawberries, thawed and drained, if fresh ones are not available.
* Replace the granola with chopped nuts or cookie crumbs for a different texture.
* Add a sprinkle of sea salt or a pinch of cinnamon to balance the sweetness.
* Create a strawberry sauce by cooking down fresh strawberries with a little sugar and lemon juice. Use this sauce as a topping or mix it with the yogurt for a strawberry-infused layer.
